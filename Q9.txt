1. What is the goal of SPHERE research program ?

2. How do they do to achieve the goal? Give detail explanation
================================
1. impact a range of healthcare needs simultaneously by employing data-fusion and pattern-recognition from a common platform of non-medical networked sensors in a home environment.

2. How to acheve SPEHERE goals by applying this:
-WP1: environment sensing (indirect) = smart home technology: interoperability of systems and data
- WP2: video monitoring (remote)= 24/7 opportunistic video analytics in the home--> detect and track body movements, extract relevant features, analyse/identify specific patterns relevant for clinicians.
- WP3 & WP4:body-area sensing and communications & energy harvesting and management (on body) = on-body, harvesting-powered wearables: PHY/MAC innovation needed, new materials and circuits needed.
- WP5:data fushion and data mining= dealing with multi-dimensional sensor data, classifying activities, visualising
	4 concept for powering medical sensors: radio frequency, inductive (for smart sensor), photovoltaic ( for specific sensors), ferroelectret
- WP6: design, deployment, & monitoring=deployment in 100 homes in Bristol. keeping SPHERE focussed on the real world. gathering a unique dataset.
